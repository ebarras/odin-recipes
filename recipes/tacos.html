Tacos

Description

Tacos are a dish made out of a flour or corn tortillia folded in half and filled with meats, sauces, vegetables, cheese, and spices

Ingredients

  • Tortillia
  • Carne Asada
  • Shredded Pepperjack Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Put the tortillia on a plate.
  2. Put the meat, sauce, cheese and veggies on the tortillia.
  3. Fold the tortillia in half.
  4. Put that taco in your face.
