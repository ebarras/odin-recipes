Hpot Dogs

Description

Ramen noodles are japanese rice noodles in a umami broth with added veggies or proteins.

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog Style Bun
  • Hot Dog
  • Mustard
  • Onion
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Add in the hot dogs. Boil roughly 2-3 minutes until heated.
  3. Add the hotdog to the bun.
  4. Add a line of mustard.
  5. Add diced onion on top of the mustard.
  6. Eat.
